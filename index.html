FAQ

Is D Day a private countdown app?

Yes. D Day is designed with absolute privacy in mind. There are no user accounts, no ads, and no data tracking. Your event countdowns and dates stay entirely on your device.

Does it include iOS Home Screen widgets?

Absolutely. You can add beautifully designed event countdown widgets directly to your iPhone or iPad Home Screen to track how many days are left until your important dates without opening the app.

How does the visual year tracker work?

Unlike a standard calendar, D Day turns your entire year into a single visual grid. It allows you to see exactly how much of the year has passed and how many days are left, helping you stay focused and make every day count.

Can I sync my events with Apple Calendar?

Yes! D Day features seamless Apple Calendar sync. You can import your existing schedule directly into the app so your event countdowns and year progress matrix are perfectly aligned.

How do I share my countdowns with friends?

You can generate and share high-resolution, custom countdown images straight to Instagram, WhatsApp, or iMessage in one tap. You can even include custom photos, checklists, and notes in your shared images.
